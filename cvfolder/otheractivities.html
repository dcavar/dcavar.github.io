Community services

LINGUIST List 2010-2018

  • Co-director (2013-2018): among others moderating, system administration (AWS servers), software development and maintanance, personnel management, relations with supporting publishers, advertisers, partners, with the co-director Malgorzata E. Cavar and our IT-staff Lwin Moe.
  • I conceptualized and realized also these services:
    • GeoLing (mapping linguistic institutions, events, and other relevant information on a global map). This is a real-time mapping of LINGUIST List posts on a global map. Between 2015 and 2017.
    • Alexa (Amazon Echo) Flash Briefing on LINGUIST List. Go to your Alexa App and set up a new Flash Briefing. You can hear updates on Jobs, Book announcements, Conference calls, etc. using Alexa. Between: 2016 and 2017.
    • LINGUIST List postings on Facebook, Google+, Twitter. This tripled the LINGUIST List reader and follower base worldwide. Between 2014 and 2017.
    • Full migration of LINGUIST List to AWS using Linux VMs. Migration of all systems to free and open source: email servers; database to PostgreSQL; web services to: Apache, Lucee, Tomcat; list server to Mailman; implementation of online donation and payment systems, etc. Between 2013 and 2017.
